Most Famous Signers

Thomas Jefferson
The principal author of the Declaration and future 3rd President of the United States.
John Adams
A strong advocate for independence, later becoming the 2nd President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
A well-known diplomat, scientist, and statesman, he played a key role in securing French support during the Revolution.
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress, famously known for his large, bold signature on the document.
Samuel Adams
A prominent leader of the American Revolution and cousin of John Adams, he was a strong advocate for independence.
Richard Henry Lee
Proposed the resolution for independence, which led to the Declaration.
Thomas Paine
While he didn't sign the Declaration itself, his pamphlet *Common Sense* was highly influential in swaying public opinion towards independence.
